How one platform carries a season across four brands

One timeline across every brand
HOKA, UGG, Teva, and AHNU each run seasonal calendars that touch design, merchandising, and marketing simultaneously. A shared platform gives each brand team its own workspace while surfacing cross-brand milestones, sample deadlines, and launch gates to leadership without a manual rollup.
Seasonal handoffs without the slip
When design approvals, line plan updates, and GTM briefs live in separate spreadsheets, delays compound across handoffs. A single source of truth means a finalized colorway in product development automatically flows into the merchandising line plan and the marketing brief, with no re-keying and no version confusion.
Repeatable GTM playbook per brand
Each brand runs a structurally similar go-to-market motion, from wholesale sell-in through DTC launch and campaign activation. A shared template lets each brand team adapt the playbook for its own season while letting the operations layer track status, blockers, and sign-offs across all four brands at once.
Visibility from brief to shelf
Executives and cross-functional partners today piece together seasonal progress from status emails and one-off decks. A connected system surfaces the real-time state of every in-flight season, which styles are locked, which are at risk, and what is launching when, without requiring a weekly all-hands to find out.

Customer Story
How TOMS connected product development to launch
Challenge
TOMS' process to manage materials selection and production across design, materials development, merchandising, creative, and leadership often required manually exporting data from one tool and importing it into another, leading to version conflicts and wasted review cycles.
Solution
TOMS built a single connected workspace that carries a product from brief through materials review, line plan sign-off, and creative handoff, eliminating the spreadsheet handoffs between functions.
Result
Design, merchandising, and creative teams now operate from the same source of truth, dramatically reducing the cycle time between a finalized colorway and a live campaign.
Why it matters for Deckers
Deckers runs this exact motion four times over, once per brand, every season. TOMS' fix for disconnected product development and merchandising handoffs maps directly onto how HOKA, UGG, Teva, and AHNU each manage their seasonal cycles. The opportunity is not just to fix one brand's workflow but to give every brand a repeatable, visible process that connects design to launch without the spreadsheet layer.
