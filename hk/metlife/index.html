Customer Story
How BlackRock unified its roadmap and cut a week-long status chase to real time
Problem
Internal data was siloed across tools like wiki, email, chat, and spreadsheets, sometimes taking a full week to check the status of a release to communicate cross-functionally or report to leadership, with little visibility into how the roadmap was prioritized.
Solution
BlackRock built a connected app powering their entire product roadmap, tracking feedback and tasks tied to goals and consolidating cross-functional communications across product, design, marketing, and business development. Leadership gets its own views, tailored to just the information it needs.
Results
With centralized communications and accurate visibility into release timelines, BlackRock increased product adoption 2x and saved 580 hours a month, with product marketing alone saving 80 hours a week from an accessible, up-to-date roadmap.
