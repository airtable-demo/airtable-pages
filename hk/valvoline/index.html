Customer Story
Schaeffler: one Content Factory across regions
Challenge
Fragmented tools across intake, planning, and execution made it hard to align regional content with global brand themes, creating version chaos and subjective agency feedback.
Solution
Schaeffler consolidated six tools into a single Content Factory on a parent-child app model, with AI automating designer assignments, scoring, and standardized agency feedback through summaries and translations.
Result
Content request cycle time dropped from 12 days to 6, teams saved over 5 hours per person each week, and IT costs fell 35% while scaling to 370 users.
Why it matters for Valvoline
A global automotive supplier aligning regional content to a global brand is the same challenge facing Valvoline Global's marketing and DASH360 teams across 140-plus countries.

Customer Story
Red Bull Media House: one global source of truth
Challenge
Red Bull Media House is distributed across dozens of channels in over 170 countries and 36 languages. Every team's workflow was siloed, with no central place to plan, produce, and distribute time-sensitive content.
Solution
They built Media Planet Hub, a single source of truth and global creative production pipeline to plan and execute every piece of content created worldwide.
Result
The hub drove transparency and collaboration across a large, distributed organization, freeing teams to focus on the work rather than chasing the status of a project.
Why it matters for Valvoline
Valvoline Global runs brand and content across 140-plus countries. A single source of truth for globally distributed teams is exactly the coordination layer this play is about.
